WHAT DOES IT INCLUDE?


  • WEEKLY SESSIONS:

    These daily activities are the secret sauce, the baby steps which are especially designed to give you bite-sized experiential learning which are simple yet profound. They bring lasting changes for enhanced HAPPINESS.

  • WEEKLY CLARITY CALLS
    Mid-week you get a clarity call from your coach to check in with you on the process and assignments. These super short calls do not let you procrastinate and keep you motivated and accountable.

  • JOURNAL FOR REFLECTIONS
    This journal helps you keep track of your thoughts and emotions. They let you capture your WOW moments and insights that keep emerging on your journey. This journey will be your friend you can keep coming back to even when the program ends. IT’s a treasure!!!

  • NEW MEDITATION EVERY WEEK
    No matter how hard we try, negative thoughts and limiting beliefs keep on coming back. This is where our eastern approach of meditation becomes significant. Whether you are a meditator or you have never meditated before, as a coach I will help you become more meditative in your day-to-day life. These meditations are unique in their approach and are specially designed to accelerate your happiness journey by 10x.
